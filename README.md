## Backpacking-Brand-Marketing-Team-Project
### Introduction  
Worked in a team of 5 running marketing campaign for a simulated backpack company against competing teams. Each week represented a quarter with 6 weeks total.  
### Initial Strategy
Decided to go with high price, high quality, "more for more" in a smaller but growing market (outdoor enthusiasts/hikers). We predicted most competitors would go for other target markets. We ended up with 2 other competitors targeting the same customer base, but our product had the highest quality and most desirable features, as well as we invested in MDF funding in high-end outdoor store, giving us the upperhand.
